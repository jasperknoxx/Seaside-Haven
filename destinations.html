Discover Paradise

Hidden gems and pristine waters of Puerto Vallarta's Bay of Banderas

Exclusive Access to Nature's Masterpiece

Puerto Vallarta's Bay of Banderas is one of Mexico's largest natural bays, offering an incredible diversity of pristine destinations accessible only by sea. Our experienced captains know every hidden cove, secluded beach, and secret snorkeling spot, ensuring your voyage reveals the bay's most treasured locations.

Los Arcos Marine Park

A breathtaking natural monument featuring towering rock formations rising from crystal-clear waters. This protected marine sanctuary offers world-class snorkeling among vibrant coral reefs, home to tropical fish, sea turtles, and manta rays. The iconic arches create dramatic backdrops for photography while providing sheltered coves perfect for swimming and relaxation.

Perfect for: Snorkeling, Photography, Marine Life

Majahuitas Beach

An untouched paradise accessible only by boat, where pristine white sand meets lush tropical jungle. This secluded sanctuary offers complete privacy for intimate gatherings, with calm, protected waters ideal for swimming and paddleboarding. The beach's natural beauty provides the perfect setting for beachside dining and sunset celebrations.

Perfect for: Privacy, Beach Picnics, Romantic Moments

Yelapa Village

A charming traditional fishing village nestled in a picturesque bay, where time moves at the rhythm of the tides. Explore cascading waterfalls, artisan shops, and authentic Mexican culture while enjoying the village's laid-back atmosphere. The protected bay offers excellent swimming conditions and stunning mountain vistas.

Perfect for: Cultural Exploration, Waterfall Hikes, Authentic Cuisine

Quimixto Falls

A hidden gem where freshwater cascades meet the sea, creating a unique ecosystem perfect for adventure and relaxation. Accessible via a scenic jungle hike or horseback ride, this natural wonder offers refreshing pools beneath towering waterfalls. The pristine beach provides an idyllic setting for picnicking and exploring.

Perfect for: Adventure, Freshwater Swimming, Nature Photography

Colomitos Beach

Mexico's smallest natural beach, a tiny crescent of golden sand embraced by dramatic cliffs and tropical vegetation. This intimate cove offers unparalleled seclusion, with calm waters perfect for snorkeling and swimming. The beach's miniature scale creates a sense of exclusive ownership, ideal for private celebrations.

Perfect for: Ultimate Privacy, Intimate Gatherings, Unique Experiences

Las Animas Beach

A vibrant beach destination where local culture meets natural beauty, offering beachfront restaurants serving fresh seafood and traditional Mexican fare. The expansive sandy beach provides ample space for beach games, volleyball, and relaxation, while the clear waters invite swimming and kayaking adventures.

Perfect for: Local Cuisine, Beach Activities, Cultural Immersion

Islas Marietas

A UNESCO Biosphere Reserve featuring the world-famous Hidden Beach, accessible through a short swim through a tunnel in volcanic rock. This protected archipelago is home to diverse bird species and pristine marine ecosystems. The islands offer exceptional diving, snorkeling, and wildlife observation opportunities.

Perfect for: World-Famous Hidden Beach, Wildlife, Eco-Tourism

Sunset Sailing Grounds

The deep waters of Banderas Bay offer unobstructed views of some of the world's most spectacular sunsets. Our captains know the perfect positioning for sunset viewing, where the Sierra Madre mountains create a dramatic backdrop as the sun paints the sky in brilliant oranges and purples, reflecting off the calm Pacific waters.

Perfect for: Romance, Photography, Evening Celebrations
