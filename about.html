Our Story

A passion for the ocean, dedication to excellence

Born from a Love of the Sea

Seaside Haven was founded on a simple yet profound belief: that every voyage should be a masterpiece of detail, privacy, and pleasure. Our journey began with a group of passionate mariners who recognized that the true magic of the ocean could only be unlocked through exceptional service and unwavering attention to detail.

What started as a dream to share the pristine beauty of Puerto Vallarta's Bay of Banderas has evolved into a premier luxury charter company, renowned for crafting bespoke maritime experiences that exceed the highest expectations. We don't just provide yacht charters; we orchestrate symphonies of indulgence on the water.

Our Philosophy

"Every voyage is a masterpiece of detail, privacy, and pleasure."

This philosophy guides every decision we make, from the selection of our pristine fleet to the training of our world-class crew members. We believe that luxury is not just about opulent amenities, but about the seamless harmony of service, expertise, and genuine care for our guests' experiences.

Our commitment extends beyond providing transportation on water. We are curators of memories, architects of adventure, and guardians of the ocean's most precious moments. Each charter is approached as a blank canvas, ready to be painted with the unique desires and dreams of our guests.

Unparalleled Expertise

Local Knowledge

Our captains and crew possess intimate knowledge of Puerto Vallarta's Bay of Banderas, from hidden coves accessible only at certain tides to the best sunset viewing points that remain untouched by crowds.

Culinary Excellence

Our onboard chefs are masters of their craft, specializing in fresh, locally-sourced ingredients transformed into gourmet experiences that complement the majesty of the ocean setting.

Safety First

Every member of our crew is certified in advanced maritime safety protocols, ensuring that your luxury experience never compromises on security and peace of mind.

Personalized Service

We pride ourselves on anticipating needs before they're expressed, creating an environment where luxury feels effortless and every detail contributes to your perfect day at sea.

Our Commitment to Excellence

At Seaside Haven, we understand that luxury is deeply personal. What constitutes the perfect day at sea for one guest may be entirely different for another. This understanding drives our commitment to bespoke service—every charter is meticulously planned and executed to reflect the unique preferences and desires of our guests.

From intimate romantic getaways for two to grand celebrations for hundreds, we approach each voyage with the same level of dedication and attention to detail. Our fleet of hand-selected yachts and catamarans represents the finest in maritime luxury, while our crew represents the gold standard in hospitality and seamanship.

We are more than a charter company; we are your partners in creating memories that will last a lifetime. Every sunset witnessed from our decks, every meal savored in the gentle ocean breeze, and every moment of serenity found in the vast blue expanse is a testament to our unwavering commitment to your complete satisfaction.

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow, our vision remains clear: to be the premier luxury yacht charter company not just in Puerto Vallarta, but recognized internationally for our exceptional service and commitment to creating transformative maritime experiences.

We are constantly evolving, incorporating the latest in luxury amenities and sustainable practices, ensuring that future generations will be able to enjoy the same pristine waters and abundant marine life that make the Bay of Banderas so special.
